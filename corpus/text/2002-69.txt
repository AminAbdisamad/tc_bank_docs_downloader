No: 2002-69
August 26, 2002

PRESS RELEASE

New web site of Central Bank of Turkey has been put in service as of August 26, 2002
to increase the accessibility.
In addition to the large amount of data on the Central Bank, markets and financial
system included in the web pages, the site contains many sections like information related
to the Bank, its publications, all announcements and statements, Governor's remarks,
library pages, banknote and painting museum.
Central Bank considers Internet as the main communication platform and in this respect
the Central Bank web site will continue to be the primary medium for all announcements,
statements and studies.
