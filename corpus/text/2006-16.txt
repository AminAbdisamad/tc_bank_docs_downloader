April 04, 2006

PRESS RELEASE
OF THE CENTRAL BANK OF TURKEY

In order to ensure smooth operation of decision-making mechanisms of the Bank, Mrs.
Çiğdem Köse, Executive Director of Markets Department of the CBRT, has been appointed
temporarily with the approval of State Minister Mr. Ali Babacan to execute the duties of Vice
Governor until a permanent appointment is made to this position, which is vacated by Mr.
Fatih Özatay due to his retirement.
