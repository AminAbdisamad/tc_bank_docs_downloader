PRESS RELEASE
25 July 2016

Briefing on Inflation Report 2016-III will be given on 26 July 2016
in Ankara
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2016-III" on
Tuesday, 26 July 2016 and Governor Murat Çetinkaya will hold a briefing on the report at 10:30 a.m.
the same day in Ankara.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
