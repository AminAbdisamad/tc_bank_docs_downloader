Briefing on Inflation Report 2020-IV will
be given on 28 October 2020 in İstanbul
23 October 2020, No. 2020-62
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2020-IV" on
Wednesday, 28 October 2020 and Governor Murat Uysal will hold a briefing on the report at
10.30 a.m. the same day at the Istanbul Office of the CBRT. The meeting will take place in person.
A live webcast of the briefing will be available here on the website as well as on the official Twitter
and YouTube accounts of the CBRT.
FOR THE ATTENTION OF MEMBERS OF THE MEDIA
Members of the press and economist who wish to attend the briefing should inform the
Bank by 4 p.m. on Monday, 26 October 2020, via an e-mail to:
basin@tcmb.gov.tr.
