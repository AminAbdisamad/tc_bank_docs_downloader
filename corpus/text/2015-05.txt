PRESS RELEASE ON CHARGING COMMISSION ON EURO
DENOMINATED ACCOUNTS HELD WITH THE CENTRAL BANK
Taking into consideration the expectations that the European Central Bank’s
implementation of negative deposit rate announced on 5 June 2014 will last for a
long period; it has been decided to apply a 20 basis point annual commission, to be
charged on daily account balances and collected on a monthly basis, on required
reserves and two days notice accounts denominated in Euro held by banks and
finance companies with the Central Bank.
The implementation of commission will be effective as of 1 February 2015.
