PRESS RELEASE ON INSUBSTANTIAL NEWS AND COMMENTS IN THE MEDIA

Recently, there have been some news and comments in the media that are
based on assumptions and not reflective of the facts regarding the Central Bank’s
policies and implementations.
It is strongly recommended not to rely on such insubstantial news and
comments that are not based on the Central Bank’s announcements and statements.
