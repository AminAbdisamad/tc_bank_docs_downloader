Press Release on Reserve Requirements  
27 May  2019, No: 201 9-22 
 
Reserve requirement ratios for FX deposits/participation funds have been increased  by 200 basis 
points fo r all maturity brackets  to support financial stability . 
As a result of this, 4,2 billion USD of FX liquidity will be withdrawn from the market.
