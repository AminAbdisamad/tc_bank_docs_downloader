PRESS RELEASE

Briefing on Inflation Report 2017-III will be given on 1 August
2017 in Ankara
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2017-III" on
Tuesday, 1 August 2017 and Governor Murat Çetinkaya will hold a briefing on the report at 10:30
a.m. the same day in Ankara.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
