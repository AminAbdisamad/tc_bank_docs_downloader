Press Release on the Academic Research Projects to be Granted
by the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey announces a financial support program for
the research projects. With the program, the Bank intends to support academic
studies in the field of the Turkish economic history, preferably on central banking and
macroeconomic issues such as inflation, banking, money, and credit.
In this context, research projects will be evaluated within the scope of the Financial
Support Program for Academic Studies. Please click on “EğitimAkademik/AÇMDP/Akademik Araştırma Projelerine Mali Destek” for further
information about the process of the program and application requirements.
