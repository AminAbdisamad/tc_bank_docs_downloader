6 February 2014

PRESS RELEASE ON THE REVISION OF THE MONTHLY PRICE
DEVELOPMENTS REPORT AND THE REAL EFFECTIVE EXCHANGE RATE
The Turkish Statistical Institute (TURKSTAT) has revised the previously
announced Consumer Price Index (CPI) for January 2014. Therefore, the Central
Bank of the Republic of Turkey has also revised its “Monthly Price Developments”
report and “Real Effective Exchange Rate” statistics.
