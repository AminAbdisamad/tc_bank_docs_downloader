PRESS RELEASE ON THE DISSEMINATION OF NON-RESIDENTS’
HOLDINGS OF SECURITIES

Non-residents’ holdings of equity and debt securities data compiled by the
Central Bank of Turkey started to be disseminated under the heading “NonResidents’ Holdings Of Securities” under the section Publications / Periodic
Publications on our website.
The definitions, advance release calendar and contact information relating to
the aforementioned weekly data can be reached at the same address.
