PRESS RELEASE ON THE “2014 RESEARCH AWARDS” TO BE GRANTED BY
THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey announces a call for the “2014
Research Awards” grant submissions. With the 2014 Research Awards, the Bank
intends to support academic studies in the field of the Turkish economy, emerging
market economies and/or central banking, and make these studies accessible to a
larger audience.
The deadline for applications is 12 May 2014, close of business. Please click
on the “Application Form” and, for further information about the awards, click on
“Announcement of the 2014 Research Awards ”.
