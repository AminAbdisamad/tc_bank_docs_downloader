Press Release on the Meeting of the 
Central Banks and  Monetary  
Authorities of the OIC Member 
Countries  
24 September  2018, No: 2018 -42 
 
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) hosted the Meeting of the Central Banks and 
Monetary Authorities of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation  (OIC)  Member Countries on 23-
25 September 2018 . 
 
Opened by the CBRT Governor Murat Çetinkaya, the meeting addressed  issues related to  global  
economic  and financial developments , and financial inclusion .
