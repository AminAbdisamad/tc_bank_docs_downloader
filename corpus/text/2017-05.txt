PRESS RELEASE

Briefing on Inflation Report 2017-I will be given on 31 January
2017 in Ankara
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2017-I" on Tuesday,
31 January 2017 and Governor Murat Çetinkaya will hold a briefing on the report at 10:30 a.m. the
same day in Ankara.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
