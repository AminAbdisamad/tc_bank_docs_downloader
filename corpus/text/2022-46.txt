Press Release on Revisions of Balance
of Payments Statistics
11 November 2022

No: 2022-46

The “Services/Travel/Credit” and “Primary Income/Compensation of Employees/Debit” items
under the “Current Account”, have been revised. As a result, the Current Account has been
updated upwards by a total of USD 26.8 billion for the period subject to revision.

As part of the revision work carried out by the Turkish Statistical Institute in cooperation with the
Ministry of Trade, the Ministry of Culture and Tourism, the Council of Higher Education, the
Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye and the Interbank Card Center to improve tourism
statistics, new data sources have been integrated into the system, methodological improvements
have been made and tourism statistics have been revised retrospectively to 2012. Following the
revision, the "Services/Travel/Credit" item under the "Current Account" has been revised upwards
by a total of USD 22.4 billion for the period from January 2012 to August 2022.
Additionally, as a result of the efforts to improve data quality and integrate new data sources into
the data compilation process, the “Primary Income/Compensation of Employees/Debit” item
under the “Current Account” item has been revised downwards by a total of USD 4.4 billion for
the period from January 2017 to August 2022.
Detailed data and information are available on the Balance of Payments Statistics page.
