Press Release on Turkish Lira Liquidity Management 
Banks’ borrowing limits at the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) 
Interbank Money Market have been reduced to TL 11 b illion to be effective as of 16 
January 2017. 
On the days deemed necessary, the amount of funding  provided by the CBRT through 
Borsa Istanbul repo markets may be limited. Banks w ill be able to meet their 
remaining liquidity needs without limits at late li quidity window funding rate at the 
end of the day.
