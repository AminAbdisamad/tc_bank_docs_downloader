Press Release on the 2nd International
Photo Contest on the theme of
“Economy and Crafts”
6 February 2019, Number: 2019-07
Fourteen winning entries and 60 selected photos from the 2nd International Photo Contest on
the theme of “Economy and Crafts” organized by the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will
be on display from 7 to 27 February 2019 at CerModern Arts Center, Ankara.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on Wednesday, 6 February 2019 at 7.00
p.m.
