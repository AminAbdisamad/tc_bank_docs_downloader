PRESS RELEASE ON INFLATION DEVELOPMENTS
MONETARY POLICY COMMITTEE MEETING

AND

1- Inflation indicators continue to improve in recent months owing to the
implementation of cautious monetary and liquidity policies.
2- The Monetary Policy Committee will assess the inflation outlook in detail at the
regular meeting which will be held on the 24th of February.
