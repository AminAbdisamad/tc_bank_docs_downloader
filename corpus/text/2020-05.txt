Briefing on Inflation Report 2020-I will
be given on 30 January 2020 in Ankara
24 January 2020, No. 2020-05

The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2020-I" on
Thursday, 30 January 2020 and Governor Murat Uysal will hold a briefing on the report at 10.30
a.m. the same day at the Head Office of the CBRT.
A live webcast of the briefing will be available here on the website as well as on the official Twitter
and YouTube accounts of the CBRT.
FOR THE ATTENTION OF MEMBERS OF THE MEDIA
Members of the press who wish to attend the briefing should inform the Bank
by 2 p.m. on Wednesday, 29 January 2020, via an e-mail to:
basin@tcmb.gov.tr.
