PRESS RELEASE
08 December 2014

Press Release on Foreign Exchange Selling Auctions
Due to heightened volatility observed in the exchange rates, the daily foreign
exchange selling auction amount will be increased from “minimum USD 20 million” to
“minimum USD 40 million”, from 9 December 2014 onwards.
