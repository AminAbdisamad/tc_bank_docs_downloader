PRESS RELEASE
18 November 2017

Press Release on Turkish Lira-Settled Forward Foreign Exchange
Sale Auctions
It has been decided to launch Turkish lira-settled forward foreign exchange sale
auctions.
The maximum total amount of foreign exchange sale position is planned to be USD 3
billion until end-2017. The auction calendar is given below. The calendar for auctions
to be held in 2018 will be announced later.

Contact: For further information, you may send an e-mail to basin@tcmb.gov.tr
