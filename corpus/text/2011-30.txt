PRESS RELEASE ON REQUIRED RESERVES  
In line with the strategic framework set out at the  Monetary Policy Committee 
Meeting held on 4 August 2011, with the aim of miti gating the fluctuations in repo 
interest rates, an amendment has been made to the C ommuniqué on Reserve 
Requirements. Accordingly, the funding from the rep o transactions will be included in 
the liabilities that are currently subject to requi red reserves by taking the average of 
daily balances between two calculation periods, ins tead of being computed bi-weekly 
on Fridays. 
The abovementioned regulation will be effective as of the calculation period of 
5 August 2011.
