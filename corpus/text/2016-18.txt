“Masterpieces from the CBRT Art Collection” on Display at Cer
Modern Arts Center
The exhibition “Masterpieces from the Art Collection of the Central Bank of the
Republic of Turkey” will be held from 6 May to 31 July 2016 at the Cer Modern Arts
Center, Ankara.
Masterpieces from the Art Collection of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey
features 69 works by 40 artists and includes examples of modern art and Turkish
painting and sculpture from the modernist period.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on 5 May 2016 at 7:00 p.m.
Venue: Cer Modern Arts Center
