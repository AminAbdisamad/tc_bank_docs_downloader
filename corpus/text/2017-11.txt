Food and Agricultural Product Markets Monitoring an d 
Evaluation Committee Convened 
The Food and Agricultural Product Markets Monitorin g and Evaluation Committee 
(the Committee) met under the presidency of Deputy Prime Minister Mehmet 
Şimşek. A summary of the agenda and decisions made is given below. 
• The Committee evaluated the signals obtained from t he Early Warning System 
and decided to take a number of product-based measu res that will yield 
results in the short-term. 
• Working sub-groups were established regarding subsi dies, standardization and 
competition-enhancing measures to boost the efficie ncy of the food supply 
chain. 
• The Ministry of Customs and Trade presented informa tion on measures and 
subsidies to minimize product losses during harvest , storage and 
transportation stages, on developments related to t he enhancement of 
licensed warehouse capacity, on expanding specializ ed commodity exchanges, 
and on other logistical arrangements. 
• The Ministry of Economy delivered a presentation on  the update of the 
Customs Union and its link to the agricultural sect or. 
• Private sector representatives presented models of good agricultural practices 
and other best practices aimed at avoiding post-har vest losses and waste. 
• The agenda and the work schedule of the Committee w ere reviewed.
