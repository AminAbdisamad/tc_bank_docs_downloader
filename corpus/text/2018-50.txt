Press Release on the Issue of TL 20 
Banknotes of E -9 Emission Group, 
Series III  
21 Decemb er 2018, No: 201 8-50 
 
As per  the authorization given to the Central Bank of the Republic  of Turkey by Law No . 1211, the 
Central Bank has decided to issue TL 20 banknotes of E-9 Emission Group , series  III, as they are 
printed.   
Compared to  the TL 20 bankn otes of  E-9 Emission Group , series  II that were put into c irculation as 
of 24 December  2012 , only the signatures o n the TL 20 bankn otes of E-9 Emission Group , series  III 
have been changed.  
The signatures on the banknotes have been rearranged  to include Governor Murat Çetinkaya’s 
and Deputy Governor Dr. Uğur Namık Küçük ’s signatures.  
TL 20 banknotes  of E-9 Emission Group , series  III will be put into  circulation as of 24 December 
2018. 
The new  banknotes, except  for the signatures, will be identical to the  series  I and II banknotes 
with regard to their dimensions, obverse and reverse  compositions, general features and 
appearances. The banknotes of series I, II and III  will be in circulation concurrently.
