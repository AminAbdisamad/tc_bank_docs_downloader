The Exhibition Tradition, Culture and Modernity: Contemporary 
Art from Turkey  to Welcome Malaysian Art Lovers  
The Islamic Arts Museum Malaysia will host the exhibition, Tradition, Culture and 
Modernity: Contemporary Art from Turkey , featuring a selection of works from the 
Art Collection of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey from 3 December 2014 to 
31 January 2015.  
The opening ce remony of the exhibition will take place on Tuesday, 2 December 2014 
at 3:00 pm local time, in the presence of Mr. Erdem Başçı, the Governor of the 
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey; H .E. Mrs. Başak Türkoğlu, the Turkish 
Ambassador in Kuala Lumpur; an d Mr. Syed Mohamad Albukhary, the Director of the 
Islamic Arts Museum Malaysia. The ceremony  will be attended by the guest of honor 
Mr. Tan Sri Dato’ Sri Dr. Ng Yen Yen, Chairman of Tourism Malaysia.
