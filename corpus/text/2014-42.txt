CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEASE
Regulations prepared by the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey regarding
activities and oversight of payment and securities settlement systems are
published in the Official Gazette.
The Law No. 6493 on Payment and Securities Settlement Systems, Payment
Services and Electronic Money Institutions is enacted by being published in the
Official Gazette No. 28690 of 27/6/2013.
In accordance with the Law No. 6493, to ensure smooth and uninterrupted
operation of payment and securities settlement systems, the Central Bank of the
Republic of Turkey is authorized for making necessary arrangements to set principles
and procedures regarding activities and oversight of the systems.
In this context, “Regulation on Activities of Payment and Securities Settlement
Systems” and “Regulation on Oversight of Payment and Securities Settlement
Systems” are enacted by being published in the Official Gazette No. 29044 of
28/6/2014.
