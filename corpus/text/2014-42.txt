CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEAS E  
Regulations prepared by the Central Bank of the Rep ublic of Turkey  regarding 
activities and oversight of payment and securities settlement systems are 
published in the Official Gazette.  
The Law No. 6493 on Payment and Securities Settleme nt Systems, Payment 
Services and Electronic Money Institutions is enact ed by being published in the 
Official Gazette No. 28690 of 27/6/2013.  
In accordance with the Law No. 6493, to ensure smoo th and uninterrupted 
operation of payment and securities settlement syst ems, the Central Bank of the 
Republic of Turkey is authorized for making necessa ry arrangements to set principles 
and procedures regarding activities and oversight o f the systems.  
In this context, “Regulation on Activities of Payme nt and Securities Settlement 
Systems” and “Regulation on Oversight of Payment an d Securities Settlement 
Systems” are enacted by being published in the Offi cial Gazette No. 29044 of 
28/6/2014.
