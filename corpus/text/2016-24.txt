Press Release on the “2016 Research Awards” to Be Granted by
the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey announces a call for the “2016 Research
Awards” grant submissions. With the 2016 Research Awards, the Bank intends to
support academic studies in the field of the Turkish economy, emerging market
economies and/or central banking, and make these studies accessible to a larger
audience.
The deadline for applications is 29 July 2016, close of business. Please click on the
“Application Form” and, for further information about the awards, click on
“Announcement of the 2016 Research Awards”.
