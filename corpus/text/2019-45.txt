The exhibition “Narrative of the
Modern” is on display in Amasya
18 November 2019, No: 2019-45
The exhibition “Narrative of the Modern - Selected Pieces from the Central Bank of the Republic
of Turkey Art Collection” will be on display from 19 November to 20 December 2019 at Saraydüzü
Kışla Binası Milli Mücadele Müzesi, Amasya.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on 18 November 2019 at 5 p.m.
Venue:

Bahçeleriçi, Büyük Ağa Cad. No. 3
