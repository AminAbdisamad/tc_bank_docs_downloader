Press Release on the Appointment of
CBRT Deputy Governor
2 September 2020, No: 2020-56
With a decree published in the Official Gazette No. 31232 dated 2 September 2020, Dr. Emrah
Şener, whose term of office ended, has been re-appointed for a second term as Deputy Governor
of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey.
