PRESS RELEASE  
15 April 2016 
Governor Erdem Başçı delivered a presentation on “B alanced 
Growth Prospects in Turkey” in Washington DC.  
Erdem Başçı, Governor of the Central Bank of the Re public of Turkey, delivered a 
presentation on “Balanced Growth Prospects in Turke y” on Friday, April 15th , 2016 in 
Washington DC, in the margins of the IMF-World Bank  Spring Meetings.  
Governor Başçı emphasized the following points: 
• The improvement in the external balance is on track . 
• Disinflation is expected to continue. 
• Measures taken so far have improved the resilience of the economy. 
• Implementation of the announced structural reforms would contribute to the 
potential growth significantly.
