No: 2014 – 02
Release Date: 23 January 2014

CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY
PRESS RELEASE

The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will publish its “Inflation Report
2014 – I” on Tuesday, 28 January 2014. Governor Erdem Başçı will present the
report at a press conference to be held at 10:00 a.m. the same day at the
Conference Hall of the Head Office of the CBRT in Ankara.
