Press Release on the Issue of E-9 Emission Group II I. Series 
Turkish Lira Banknotes  
Within the framework of the authorization given to the Central Bank of the Republic 
of Turkey by Law No: 1211, the Central Bank has dec ided to issue E-9 Emission Group 
III. series Turkish lira banknotes, as they are pri nted. In comparison with the E-9 
Emission Group II. series TL 100, TL 20 and TL 10 b anknotes that have been in 
circulation since 24 December 2012 and the E-9 Emis sion Group II. series TL 200, TL 
50 and TL 5 banknotes that have been in circulation  since 8 April 2013, only the 
signatures of the following denominations have been  changed.  
The signatures on the banknotes have been amended a s follows;  
• on TL 200 banknotes Deputy Governor Erkan Kilimci ’s,  
• on TL 10 banknotes Deputy Governor Murat Uysal’s,  
• on TL 5 banknotes Deputy Governor Erkan Kilimci’s ,  
along with Governor Murat Çetinkaya’s signature.  
The E-9 Emission Group III. series TL 200, TL 10 an d TL 5 banknotes will be put into 
circulation as of 27 March 2017.   
The above mentioned banknotes, apart from the signa tures, will be identical to the II. 
series banknotes with regard to their dimensions, o bverse and reverse compositions, 
general features and appearances. The I., II. and I II. series banknotes will be in 
circulation concurrently.  
Another announcement regarding denominations of III . series TL 100, TL 50 and TL 20 
will be made at a later date.
