Press Release on Reserve 
Requirements  and Maintenance of  
Turkish Lira-Denominated Securities 
for Foreign Currency Liabilities  
10 June  2022 No: 2022-28 
 
On 26 May 2022 , the Monetary Policy Committee had announce d that the collateral and liquidity 
policy actions, of which the review process was finalized, would be implemented . 
Accordingly, a s a complementary step to the action increasing the weight of the Turkish lira fixed -
rate securities  in the collateral pool that will become effective on 24 June, b anks will maintain 
additional Turkish lira long -term fixed -rate securities  for foreign currency deposits/participation 
funds . The aim of this regulation  is to increas e the effec tiveness of the monetary policy within the 
scope of the liraization strategy.  The first maintenance period will start  on 29 July. 
With a view to supporting financial stability , the reserve requirement ratio for Turkish lira -
denominated commercial cash loan s, which  was announced as 10 % in the press release dated 23 
April  2022 , has been increased to 20 %. The increase will be effective from the maintenance  period 
starting on 8 July.
