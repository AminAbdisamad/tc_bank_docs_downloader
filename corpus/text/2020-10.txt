Press Release on the Issue of E9 Series,
Version IV Turkish Lira Banknotes
28 February 2020, No: 2020-10
As per the authorization given to the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey by Law No. 1211, the
E9 series, version IV banknotes in denominations of 200, 20 and 5 will be put into circulation as of
2 March 2020.
The 200, 20 and 5 TL banknotes of version IV will now carry, along with Governor Murat Uysal’s
signature, the signatures of the following authorities in the respective order:
•
•
•

Deputy Governor Dr. Emrah Şener,
Deputy Governor Dr. Oğuzhan Özbaş,
Deputy Governor Dr. Uğur Namık Küçük.

Except for the signatures, the new banknotes will be identical to version I, II and III banknotes
with regard to their dimensions, obverse and reverse compositions, general features and
appearances.
Version I, II, III and IV banknotes will be in circulation concurrently. An announcement regarding
version IV 100, 50 and 10 TL banknotes will be made at a later date.
