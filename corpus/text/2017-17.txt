31 March 2017 
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey to host the Meeting of 
Central Bank Governors’ Club of Central Asia, Black Sea Region 
and Balkan Countries 
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will host the Meeting of Central Bank 
Governors’ Club of Central Asia, Black Sea Region and Balkan Countries, on 2-4 April 
2017 in Antalya.  
The main themes of the meeting have been set as monetary policy and financial 
stability, along with enhancement of economic resilience. Besides, presentations will 
be delivered on recent economic and financial developments in member countries.  
The meeting will be closed to the press.
