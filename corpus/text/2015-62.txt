Briefing on Inflation Report 2015-IV will be given on 28 October
2015 in İstanbul
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report
2015-IV" on Wednesday, 28 October 2015 and Governor Erdem Başçı will hold a
briefing on the report at 10:30 a.m. the same day in İstanbul.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
Venue: Wyndham Grand İstanbul Levent
Büyükdere Caddesi No:177-183
34394 Şişli/ İstanbul
