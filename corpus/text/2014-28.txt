CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEASE
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will host an exhibition on
“Aesthetic Heritage: Calligraphy – Ebru (Marbling) – Sedefkârlık (Mother-ofPearl Inlay)”.
The exhibition, “Aesthetic Heritage: Calligraphy - Ebru - Sedefkârlık”, featuring a
selection of works from the Central Bank’s Art Collection will be held from 10 May to
17 July 2014 in Ankara, at the Art Gallery of the Central Bank of the Republic of
Turkey.
The exhibition displaying works of art by inlay artist Salih Balakbabalar and marbling
master Alparslan Babaoğlu on the theme of calligraphy will be opened on Friday, 9
May 2014 at 6:30 p.m.
Detailed information on the Art Collection of the Central Bank of the Republic of
Turkey is available at: http://www.tcmb.gov.tr/sanat/sanatindex_eng.html
