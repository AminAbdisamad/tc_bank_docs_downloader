PRESS RELEASE ON
THE INTERIM MONETARY POLICY COMMITTEE MEETING

The Monetary Policy Committee will meet on 28 January 2014, Tuesday
evening to discuss recent developments and take the necessary policy measures for
price stability. The decision will be announced at 24:00 hours. The press release will
be posted on the CBRT website.
