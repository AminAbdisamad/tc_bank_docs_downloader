CBRT announces winners of 2015 Research Awards. 
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) h as identified six winners of the 
"2015 Research Awards" that are granted to support academic studies in the fields of 
the Turkish economy, emerging market economies and/ or central banking, and to 
make these studies accessible to a larger audience.   
At the end of the evaluation process: 
• Gazi İshak Kara, has been granted first prize and a n award of TL 15,000.00 
for his application for the study of “Systemic Risk , International Regulation, 
and the Limits of Coordination”. 
• Sinem Hatice Ayhan, has been granted second prize a nd an award of TL 
7,500.00 for her application for the study of “Evid ence of Added Worker 
Effect from the 2008 Economic Crisis”. 
• Moazzam Farooq, has been granted third prize and an  award of TL 5,000.00 
for his application for the study of “Of Religion a nd Redemption: Evidence 
from Default on Islamic Loans”. 
• Ünal Töngür, has been granted third prize and an aw ard of TL 5,000.00 for 
his application for the study of “Patterns of Firm Growth in the Turkish 
Manufacturing Industry”. 
• H. Özlem Dursun - De Neef, has been granted an acad emic encouragement 
award and a prize of TL 1,500.00 for her applicatio n for the study of “The 
Transmission of Liquidity Shocks to the Real Econom y”. 
• Bilge Karataş, has been granted an academic encoura gement award and a 
prize of TL 1,500.00 for her application for the st udy of “Three Sisters: The 
Interlinkage Between Sovereign Debt, Currency, and Banking Crises”.
