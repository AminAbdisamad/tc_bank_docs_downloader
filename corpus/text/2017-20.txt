PRESS RELEASE

Briefing on Inflation Report 2017-II will be given on 28 April 2017
in İstanbul
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2017-II" on Friday,
28 April 2017 and Governor Murat Çetinkaya will hold a briefing on the report at 10:30 a.m. the same
day in İstanbul.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
