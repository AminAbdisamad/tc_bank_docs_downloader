Governor Erdem Başçı’s Presentation on "Balanced Growth
Prospects in Turkey" in Washington DC
Governor Erdem Başçı delivered a presentation on “Balanced Growth Prospects in
Turkey” to the US Chamber of Commerce on Thursday, 09 October 2014 at 18:30
p.m. local time in Turkey.
The following points are emphasized in Governor Başçı’s presentation:
•
Exports remain supportive of balanced growth in spite of weakening global
demand.
•
Tight monetary policy stance and macroprudential measures are keeping loan
growth rates at reasonable levels.
•

Declining commodity prices are expected to limit upside risks on inflation.
