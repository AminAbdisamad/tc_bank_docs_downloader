3 August 2011

PRESS RELEASE ON
THE INTERIM MONETARY POLICY COMMITTEE MEETING

The Monetary Policy Committee will hold an interim meeting on 4 August
2011, Thursday to evaluate recently increasing concerns over the sovereign debt
problems in some European countries and the global growth outlook. The Committee
will meet between 10:00 am-12:00 pm and issue a press release at 12:00 pm to
announce its decision. The press release will be posted on the CBRT website.
