Communication Practices of the Central Bank of the Republic of 
Turkey  
The Central Bank of Republic of Turkey has revised its communication policy and the 
following arrangements have been introduced:  
• Meetings on technical issues will be held with inve stors and analysts.  
• Regular meetings will be organized with investors i n financial centers abroad.  
• Meetings will be held with chambers of industry and  commerce and other 
corporate sector representatives to provide a platf orm for an exchange of views.  
• Periodic meetings will be organized with representa tives of media institutions 
and the financial press.  
• The monthly meetings with bank economists will now be held when deemed 
necessary, as the need for these meetings will be r educed with these new 
arrangements.  
Briefings on Inflation Reports and other communicat ion practices will continue 
unchanged.  
The principles of implementation will be shared sep arately with the relevant parties.
