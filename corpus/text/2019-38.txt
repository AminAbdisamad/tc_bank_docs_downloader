CBRT has revized FX Reserve
Requirement ratios.
20 September 2019, No: 2019-38
To support financial stability, reserve requirement ratios for FX deposits/participation funds have
been increased by 100 basis points for all maturity brackets. As a result of these revisions
approximately USD 2.1 billion of FX liquidity will be withdrawn from the market.
The revized ratios will be effective from the calculation period of 20 September 2019 with the
maintenance period starting on 4 October 2019.
