Briefing on Inflation Report 2019-III will
be given on 31 July 2019 in Ankara
26 July 2019, No: 2019-30
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2019-III" on
Wednesday, 31 July 2019 and Governor Murat Uysal will hold a briefing on the report at 10:30
a.m. the same day at the Head Office of the CBRT.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
