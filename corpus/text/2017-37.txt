The CBRT hosted the Meeting of the Central Banks and 
Monetary Authorities of the OIC Member Countries 
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey hosted the Meeting of the Central Banks 
and Monetary Authorities of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation Member 
Countries on 21-22 September 2017, in Bodrum.   
The main themes of the meeting were global and financial developments, fostering 
stable and inclusive financial systems and financial stability.
