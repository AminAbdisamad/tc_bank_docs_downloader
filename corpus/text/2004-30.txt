THE CBRT PRESS RELEASE  
ON  
TURKISH LIRA DEPOSIT BUYING AUCTIONS PROGRAM FOR AUGUST 
2004  
In August 2004, the Central Bank will continue to conduct Turkish lira deposit buying 
auctions with a standard four -week maturity, as a supplement to its existing instruments at 
Interbank Money Market with the aim of boosting its efforts to sterilize the excess Turkish 
lira liquidity  
As in July 2004, Turkish lira deposit buying aucti ons with a standard four -week 
maturity will be held on every Wednesday. There will be no upper limit at each auction.
