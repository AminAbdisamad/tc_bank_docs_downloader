Press Release on the Swap Amendment
Agreement between CBRT and Qatar
Central Bank
20 May 2020, No: 2020-29
The swap agreement, which was signed on 17 August 2018 between the Central Bank of the
Republic of Turkey (CBRT) and Qatar Central Bank (QCB), was amended. With the swap
amendment agreement, the overall limit has been increased from USD 5 billion equivalent of
Turkish lira and Qatari riyal to USD 15 billion equivalent of Turkish lira and Qatari riyal. The core
objectives of the agreement are to facilitate bilateral trade in respective local currencies and to
support financial stability of the two countries.
