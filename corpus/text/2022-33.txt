Governor Şahap Kavcıoğlu will hold a briefing on Inflation Report 202 2-III at 10.30 a.m. on 
Thursday, 28 July 2022  at the Head Office of the CBRT. The meeting will take place in person.  
A live webcast of the Turkish -language briefing will be available  on the  Twitter  and YouTube  
accounts of the CBRT and with simultaneous  English translation  here  on the CBRT website.  
Members of the press and economists who wish to follow the briefing should inform the Bank 
by 2 p.m. on Wednesday, 27 July 2022 , via an e -mail  to: basin@tcmb.gov.tr.  
Briefing Program:  
10.30 a.m. – 11.00 a.m.: Governor Şahap Kavcıoğlu’s presentation on Inflation Report 202 2-III 
11.00 a.m. – 11.30 a.m.: Q&A session
