The Exhibition “Narration, Place and
Time” on Display in Ankara
3 July 2019, No: 2019-27
The exhibition “Narration, Place and Time”, featuring selected pieces from the Central Bank of the
Republic of Turkey Art Collection, will be on display from 4 July to 30 September 2019 at Erimtan
Archaeology and Arts Museum, Ankara.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on 3 July 2019 at 6.30 p.m.
Venue:
Erimtan Archaeology and Arts Museum
Kale Mahallesi, Gözcü Sokak No.10
Altındağ, Ankara
