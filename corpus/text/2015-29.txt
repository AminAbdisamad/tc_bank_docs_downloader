PRESS RELEASE
22 April 2015

Press Release on Remuneration of Turkish Lira Required
Reserves
The remuneration rate for the required reserves maintained in Turkish liras has been
raised by 50 basis points.
The implementation will start as of the 8 May 2015 maintenance period.
Implementation principles will continue as stated in the press release dated 21
October 2014.
