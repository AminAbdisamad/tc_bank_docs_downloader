PRESS RELEASE ON GOVERNOR BAŞÇI’S PRESENTATION
ON “MONETARY POLICIES” IN DENİZLİ

Governor Erdem Başçı will give a conference presentation on “Monetary
Policies” on Tuesday, 24 September 2013 at 10:00 hours at the Meeting Hall of the
Congress and Culture Center at Pamukkale University in Denizli.
The conference will be organized jointly by the Denizli Chamber of Commerce,
the Denizli Chamber of Industry and Dünya Newspaper.
