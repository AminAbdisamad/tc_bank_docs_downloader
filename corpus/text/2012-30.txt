 
 
 
PRESS RELEASE ON THE MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING WITH THE 
EUROPEAN CENTRAL BANK 
 
A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed between the Central 
Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) and the European Central Bank (ECB) today 
in Frankfurt, Germany by Dr. Erdem Başçı, Governor of the CBRT and Mario Draghi, President of the ECB.  
The MoU lays the ground for continued cooperation in the field of central 
banking through regular dialogue at technical and policy level and possible staff exchanges.  No: 2012 – 30 Release date:  4 July 2012
