Press Release on the Postponement of
the 88th Ordinary General Assembly
Meeting of the CBRT
19 March 2020, No: 2019-17
In consideration of the measures taken against the COVID-19 outbreak, the 88th Ordinary General
Assembly Meeting of the CBRT to be held on 20 March 2020, has been postponed and will be
scheduled for a later date.
