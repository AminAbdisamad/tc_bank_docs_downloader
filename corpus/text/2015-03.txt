PRESS RELEASE
21 January 2015

Briefing on Inflation Report 2015-I will be given on 27 January
2015 in Ankara.
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report
2015-I" on Tuesday, 27 January 2015 and Governor Erdem Başçı will hold a briefing
on the report at 10:30 a.m. the same day at the Head Office of the CBRT in Ankara.
Venue: Türkiye Cumhuriyet Merkez Bankası
İdare Merkezi
İstiklal Cad. 10 Ulus, 06100 Ankara
