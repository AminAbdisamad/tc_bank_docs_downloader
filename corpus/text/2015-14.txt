Press Release on Foreign Exchange Selling Auctions  
The amount of foreign exchange selling auctions wil l be set on a daily basis 
depending on the conditions in the foreign exchange  market starting from 27 
February 2015. 
1)  The minimum amount of the auction for the following  day will be 
announced by CBT at 17.20 through Reuters CBTQ, Blo omberg CBT/ 
Foreign Exchange Auctions and Anadolu Agency DV007 pages.   
2)  On days when deemed necessary due to excessive vola tility, the FX selling 
auction amount will be increased up to 50 percent a bove the pre-
announced minimum amount. The auction amount will b e announced as 
usual at 16.30 local time on the same pages.
