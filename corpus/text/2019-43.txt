Briefing on Inflation Report 2019 -IV will 
be given on  31 October  2019  in Istanbul  
25 October  2019, No. 2019 -43 
 
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report  2019 -IV" on 
Thursday , 31 October  2019  and Governor Murat Uysal will hold  a briefing on the report at 10. 30 
a.m. the same day at the Istanbul Branch of the CBRT.   
 
A live webcast of the briefing will be available here  on the website as well as on the official Twitter  
and YouTube  accounts of the CBRT .
