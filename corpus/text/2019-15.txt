Press Release on the Election of the
CBRT Board Members
17 April 2019, No: 2019-15
Mr. Hayrettin Demircan and Mr. Ahmet Keçeci, who were elected to serve as Members of the Board
at the General Assembly Meeting of the CBRT held on 15 March 2019, will not be able to take office.
Therefore, as per the articles of the CBRT Law and the Turkish Commercial Code, the CBRT Board
has decided that Ms. Başak Tanınmış Yücememiş and Mr. Mehmet Aktaş are elected to serve as
Members of the Board effective from 1 May 2019.
