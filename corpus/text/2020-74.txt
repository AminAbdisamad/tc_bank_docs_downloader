18 December  2020 , No: 2020-74 
 
A Memorandum of Understanding was signed between the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey 
and the Central Bank of the Republic of Azerbaijan on 14  December 2020, laying the groundwork 
for continued cooperation in the field of central banking .  
 
In the framework of the MoU, the two central banks aspire to carry out activities to foster  
cooperation in the field of central banking .
