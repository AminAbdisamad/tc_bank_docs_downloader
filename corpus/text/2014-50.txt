PRESS RELEASE ON FOREIGN EXCHANGE SELLING AUCTIONS  
  
Taking into account the favorable developments in the balance of payments, 
the daily foreign exchange selling auction amount will be decreased from “minimum 
USD 20 million” to “minimum USD 10 million”, from 25 July 2014 onwards.
