Press Release on Turkish Lira Liquidity
Management
2 November 2020, No: 2020-65
In the framework of the tightening steps taken under liquidity management, effective from 3
November 2020, banks’ borrowing limits at the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT)
Interbank Money Market will be reduced to zero, and overnight repo transactions via the
quotation method against TL-denominated lease certificate in the scope of Open Market
Operations will be suspended.
In pursuit of the price stability and the financial stability objectives, all necessary instruments
within the framework of monetary policy and liquidity management will continue to be used
decisively.
