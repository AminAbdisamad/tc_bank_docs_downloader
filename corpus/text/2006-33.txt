PRESS RELEASE  
ON 
THE FOREIGN EXCHANGE SELLING AUCTIONS   
 
 
In the press release of 26 June 2006 on “Foreign Exchange Selling and Ne w Turkish 
Lira Deposit Buying Auctions”, the Central Bank of Turkey announced that it would hold 
foreign exchange selling auctions on the days it deems necessary and would inform the public 
of the amount of the auction at 10.00 a.m.  
Within this framework, conducting a foreign exchange selling auction i s deemed 
unnecessary on 28 June 2006.
