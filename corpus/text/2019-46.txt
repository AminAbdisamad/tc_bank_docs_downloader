www.tcmb.gov.tr  Press Release on the Swap Amendment 
Agreement  between CBRT and Qatar 
Central Bank   
26 November  2019, No: 2019 -46 
The swap agreeement , which was signed between the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey 
(CBRT) and Qatar Central Bank (QCB) on 17 August 2018, was amen ded in a ceremony  on 25 
November 2019, in Doha.  
The core objective s of the agreement  are to facilitate bilateral trade in respective local currencies  
and to support financial stability of the two countries.   With the swap amendment agreeme nt, the 
overall limit has been increased from USD 3 billion equivalent of Turkish lira and Qatari riyal to 
USD 5 billion equivalent of Turkish lira and Qatari riyal.
