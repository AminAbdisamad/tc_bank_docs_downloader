Press Release on the Issue of TRY 20 
and TRY 5 banknotes of E9 Series, 
Version V  
29 September  2021  No: 2021 -43 
 
As per  the authorization given to the Central Bank of the Republic  of Turkey by Law No . 1211, the 
E9 series, version V banknotes  in denominations of TRY 20 and TRY 5 will be  put into  circulation 
as of 29 Septembe r 2021. 
Compared to TRY 20 and TRY 5 banknotes of E9 series, version I, II, III and IV in circulation,  the 
signatures on TRY 20 and TRY 5 banknotes  of version V have been rearranged to include 
Governor Prof. Şahap Kavcıoğlu ’s signature as well as the signatures of the following authorities 
in the respective order:  
 Deputy Governor Prof. Semih Tümen,  
 Deputy Gover nor Dr. Uğur Namık Küçük.   
Except for the signatures , the new banknotes will be identical to version I, II, III and IV banknotes 
with regard to their dimensions, obverse and reverse  compositions, general features and 
appearances.  
Version  I, II, III, IV and V banknotes will be circulat ed concurrently.  An announce ment regarding 
version V TRY 100  and TRY 50 banknotes will  be made at a later date.
