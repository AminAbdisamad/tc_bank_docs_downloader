Briefing on Inflation Report 2016-II will be given on 26 April 2016
in İstanbul
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report
2016-II" on Tuesday, 26 April 2016 and Governor Murat Çetinkaya will hold a briefing
on the report at 10:30 a.m. the same day in İstanbul.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
Venue: Hilton İstanbul Bomonti Hotel
Silahşör Caddesi No:42
Bomonti, Şişli/ İstanbul
Speech on "Inflation Report 2016-I" can be accessed here.
