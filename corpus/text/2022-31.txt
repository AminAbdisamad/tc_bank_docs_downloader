Press Release on the Replacement of
the SSL Certificate of the CBRT Website
14 July 2022

No: 2022-31

The SSL certificate used for secure access to our Bank's website (www.tcmb.gov.tr) will be replaced
on Friday, 15 July 2022 at 11 p.m. Users who pull data from the site should check their access after
the replacement, and add the new certificates found here to their systems in case of any problem.
