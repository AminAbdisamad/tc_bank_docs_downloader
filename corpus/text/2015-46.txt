PRESS RELEASE
27 July 2015

Briefing on Inflation Report 2015-III will be given on 30 July
2015 in Ankara.
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report
2015-III" on Thursday, 30 July 2015 and Governor Erdem Başçı will hold a briefing on
the report at 10:30 a.m. the same day at the Head Office of the CBRT in Ankara.
A live webcast of the event will be available here.
Venue: Türkiye Cumhuriyet Merkez Bankası
İdare Merkezi
İstiklal Cad. 10 Ulus, 06100 Ankara
