The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey Grants the
“2017 Research Awards”
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey announces a call for the “2017 Research
Awards” grant submissions. With the 2017 Research Awards, the Bank intends to
support academic studies in the fields of the Turkish economy, emerging market
economies and/or central banking, and make these studies accessible to a larger
audience.
The deadline for applications is 23 June 2017, close of business. Please click on the
“Application Form” and, for further information about the awards, click on
“Announcement of the 2017 Research Awards”.
