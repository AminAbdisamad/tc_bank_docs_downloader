Press Release on the Appointment of
New Governor
7 November 2020, No: 2020-66
With a decree published in Official Gazette No.31297 dated 7 November 2020, Mr. Naci Ağbal has
been appointed as Governor of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey.
At the takeover ceremony, Governor Ağbal thanked Murat Uysal and Mr. Uysal wished Governor
Ağbal all the best and every success in his new post.
