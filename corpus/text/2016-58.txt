PRESS RELEASE
2 December 2016

The Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy for 2017 will be
announced in a Press Conference on December 6, 2016
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will release “the Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy
for 2017” document on Tuesday, December 6, 2016 and Governor Murat Çetinkaya will present the
policy text at 10:30 a.m. the same day in Ankara.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
