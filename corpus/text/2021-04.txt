Press Release on International
Reserves and Foreign Currency
Liquidity Table
27 January 2021

No: 2021-04

The International Reserves and Foreign Currency Liquidity table is published on the CBRT’s
website under Statistics/ International Reserves and Foreign Currency Liquidity on a monthly
basis. In order to provide more timely data to the public, starting from 28 January 2021, this table
will also be released on a weekly basis on Thursdays at 2.30 p.m.
