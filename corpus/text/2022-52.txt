Press Release on Monetary and
Exchange Rate Policy for 2023
23 December 2022

No: 2022-52

The Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye publicly announces the monetary and exchange rate
policy that it will pursue in the following year via the Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy text it
releases every year in December.
Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy for 2023 will be released at 10.00 a.m. on Friday, 30
December 2022.

For further information, you may send an e-mail to basin@tcmb.gov.tr.
