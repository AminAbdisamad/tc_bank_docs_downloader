PRESS RELEASE
24 November 2016

Press Release on Reserve Requirements
Foreign exchange reserve requirement ratios have been decreased by 50 basis points
for all maturity brackets. With this revision, approximately 1.5 billion US dollars of
liquidity will be provided to the financial system.
