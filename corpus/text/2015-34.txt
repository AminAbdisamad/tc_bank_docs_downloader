Press Release On The “2015 Research Awards” To Be Granted
By The Central Bank Of The Republic Of Turkey
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey announces a call for the “2015 Research
Awards” grant submissions. With the 2015 Research Awards, the Bank intends to
support academic studies in the field of the Turkish economy, emerging market
economies and/or central banking, and make these studies accessible to a larger
audience.
The deadline for applications is 13 July 2015, close of business. Please click on the
“Application Form” and, for further information about the awards, click on
“Announcement of the 2015 Research Awards”.
