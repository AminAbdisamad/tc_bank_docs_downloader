Press Release on the CBRT Interbank 
Money Market Borrowing Limits  
29 August  2018, No: 2018 -34 
 
It was announced in the Press Release on Financial Markets of 13 August 2018 that in the 
framework of the intraday and overnight  standing facilities, the Central Bank w ould  provide the 
banks with all the liquidity t hat they needed .  
 
In the light of recent  evaluations, the CBRT has decided that , to be effective from 29 August 2018,  
the banks’ borrowing limits  for overnight transactions at the Interbank Money Market established 
within the CBRT  would be  twice the limits applicable bef ore 13 August 2018 .
