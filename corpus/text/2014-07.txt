No: 2014 – 07 Release Date: 29 January 2014
 
 
PRESS RELEASE ON ENDING THE ADDITIONAL MONETARY TIGHTENING POLICY
 
 
In line with the simplification decision taken at the Monetary Policy Committee Meeting of
28 January 2014, additional monetary tightening policy announced with CBRT’s Press Release of
11 June 2013 numbered 2013-25 will be ended as of today.
