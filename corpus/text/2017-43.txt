Opening of the International Photography Contest Exhibition
“Economy and the Human Being”
The exhibition featuring 10 award-winning photographs and 100 selected
photographs from the international photography contest “Economy and the Human
Being” organized by the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will be on display
from 8 November to 21 November 2017 at CerModern Arts Center, Ankara.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on Tuesday, 7 November 2017
at 7.00 p.m., in the presence of Governor Murat Çetinkaya.
Venue: CerModern Arts Center
