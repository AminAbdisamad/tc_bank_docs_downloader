Press Release on the Turkish Lira Liquidity Management
With the aim of supporting efficient liquidity management of banks, the haircut/discount rates of
Turkish lira and foreign currency denominated collaterals pledged against Turkish lira transactions
have been changed by taking into account of their types and maturities. With this adjustment,
approximately 6 billion Turkish lira-worth of pledged collaterals are expected to be released.
New implementation will be effective as of 28 July 2016.
