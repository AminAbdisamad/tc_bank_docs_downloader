Press Release on the Turkish Lira Liquidity Managem ent  
With the aim of supporting efficient liquidity mana gement of banks, the haircut/discount rates of 
Turkish lira and foreign currency denominated colla terals pledged against Turkish lira transactions 
have been changed by taking into account of their t ypes and maturities.  With this adjustment, 
approximately 6 billion Turkish lira-worth of pledg ed collaterals are expected to be released. 
New implementation will be effective as of 28 July 2016.
