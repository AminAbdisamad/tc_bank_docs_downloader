12 March 2015

The Exhibition “From Red to Blue Contemporary Turkish
Painting” to Welcome Art Lovers in Çanakkale
Commemorating the 100th anniversary of Çanakkale Victory and Çanakkale Land
Battles, the Çanakkale Ceramics Museum will host the exhibition, “From Red to Blue
Contemporary Turkish Painting”, featuring a selection of works from the Art
Collection of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey from 18 March to 28 April
2015.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on Wednesday, 18 March
2015 at 18.00 hours, in the presence of Mr. Erdem Başçı, Governor of the Central
Bank of the Republic of Turkey; Mr. Ülgür Gökhan, Mayor of Çanakkale; and Ms. Övgü
Gökdağ, Director of the Çanakkale Ceramics Museum.
Venue: Çanakkale Ceramics Museum, Çanakkale
Cevat Paşa Mah. Kaya Sokak No: 33-35 17100 Çanakkale
