Press Release on Swap Agreement
Between CBRT and Qatar Central Bank
20 August 2018, No: 2018-33
The Central Bank of Turkey (CBRT) and Qatar Central Bank (QCB) signed a Swap Agreement on
17 August 2018 in Doha, Qatar.
The swap agreement has been signed over Turkish lira and Qatari Riyal and concluded with an
overall limit of 3 billion USD. Core objective of the agreement is to facilitate bilateral trade in
respective local currencies and support financial stability of the two countries.
