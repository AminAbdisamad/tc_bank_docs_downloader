CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEAS E  
The 600th anniversary of the establishment of diplo matic relations between 
Turkey and Poland to be celebrated with a series of  events  
The year 2014 marks the 600th anniversary of the es tablishment of diplomatic 
relations between Turkey and Poland and will be cel ebrated with various events in 
both countries. As part of the programme of events,  the exhibition The Orient, 
Modernity and Art, featuring a selection of works from the Art Collect ion of the 
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey, will be on display from 26 June to 21 
September 2014 in Warsaw, at the Museum of King Jan  III’s Palace at Wilanów.  
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take pl ace on Wednesday, 25 June 2014 
in the presence of Erdem Ba şçı, the Governor of the Central Bank of Turkey; Yus uf 
Ziya Özcan, the Ambassador of the Republic of Turke y in Warsaw; and Pawel 
Jaskanis, the Director of the Museum of King Jan III’s Palace at Wilanów . The 
opening ceremony will be preceded by a presentation  on Turkish Art through the 
Ages  by Prof. Talat Halman and a recital organized by t he Republic of Turkey 
Ministry of Culture and Tourism. 
Additionally, the National Bank of Poland and the C entral Bank of the Republic of 
Turkey will host a joint conference on Poland’s and Turkey’s Transition: 
Achievements and Challenges Ahead  on Thursday, 26 June 2014, at 9:00 a.m. The 
conference will be opened with remarks by Marek Bel ka, the President of the 
National Bank of Poland, and Erdem Ba şçı, the Governor of the Central Bank of 
Turkey.
