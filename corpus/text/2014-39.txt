CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEASE
The 600th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between
Turkey and Poland to be celebrated with a series of events
The year 2014 marks the 600th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic
relations between Turkey and Poland and will be celebrated with various events in
both countries. As part of the programme of events, the exhibition The Orient,
Modernity and Art, featuring a selection of works from the Art Collection of the
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey, will be on display from 26 June to 21
September 2014 in Warsaw, at the Museum of King Jan III’s Palace at Wilanów.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on Wednesday, 25 June 2014
in the presence of Erdem Başçı, the Governor of the Central Bank of Turkey; Yusuf
Ziya Özcan, the Ambassador of the Republic of Turkey in Warsaw; and Pawel
Jaskanis, the Director of the Museum of King Jan III’s Palace at Wilanów. The
opening ceremony will be preceded by a presentation on Turkish Art through the

Ages by Prof. Talat Halman and a recital organized by the Republic of Turkey
Ministry of Culture and Tourism.
Additionally, the National Bank of Poland and the Central Bank of the Republic of
Turkey will host a joint conference on Poland’s and Turkey’s Transition:
Achievements and Challenges Ahead on Thursday, 26 June 2014, at 9:00 a.m. The
conference will be opened with remarks by Marek Belka, the President of the
National Bank of Poland, and Erdem Başçı, the Governor of the Central Bank of
Turkey.
