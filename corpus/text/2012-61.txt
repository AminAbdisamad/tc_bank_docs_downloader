No: 2012 – 61 21 December 2012  
 
 
PRESS RELEASE ON THE ISSUING OF E- 9 EMISSION GROUP II. SERIES 
TURKISH LIRA BANKNOTES  
 
Within the framework of the authorization given to the Central Bank of the 
Republic of Turkey by Law No.1211 , the Central Bank has decided to issue E -9 
Emission Group II. series Turkish lira banknotes, as they are printed. In comparison 
to the E -9 Emission Group I. series Turkish lira banknotes that have been in  
circulation since January 1, 2009, only the signat ures of the following denominations 
have been changed.  
The signatures on the banknotes are rearranged as;  
• for TL 100 banknotes Deputy Governor Necati Şahin’s,  
• for TL 20 banknotes Deputy Governor Murat Çetinkaya’s,  
• for TL 10 banknotes Deputy Governor Necati Şahin’s,  
along with Governor Erdem Başçı’s signature.  
The E -9 Emission Group II. series TL 100, TL 20 and TL 10 banknotes are 
planned to be put into circulation as of December 24, 2012.  
The above mentioned banknotes, apart from the signatures, will be identical to 
the I. series banknotes with regard to their dimensions, obverse and reverse compositions, general features and appearances. The I. and II. series banknotes will 
concurrently be in circulation.  
Another announcement regarding denominations of TL 200, TL 50 and TL 5 
will be made at a later date.
