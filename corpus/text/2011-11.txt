PRESS RELEASE ON MEETINGS WITH BANKS' ECONOMISTS

As part of our communications strategy, a series of meetings with banks’ economists
will be organized throughout the year with participation of the senior management in
order to address meeting requests. The meetings will be closed to the press.
The first of these meetings will be held on May 4, 2011 in Istanbul. Participants are
required to submit their requests to our Bank via e-mail to investor@tcmb.gov.tr no
later than the close of business on April 29, 2011.
