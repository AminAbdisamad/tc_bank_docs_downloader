Press Release on Monetary and
Exchange Rate Policy for 2021
4 December 2020, No: 2020-72
The CBRT publicly announces the monetary and exchange rate policy that it will pursue in the
following year via the Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy text it releases every year in December.
“Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy for 2021” will be released at 10.30 am on Wednesday, 16
December 2020.
