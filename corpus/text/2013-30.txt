PRESS RELEASE ON FOREIGN EXCHANGE SELLING AUCTIONS TO BE 
HELD ON THE DAYS WITH REGULAR FUNDING 
 
In line with the Monetary Policy Committee decision  held on 18 June 
2013, the composition of the liquidity provided by the Central Bank of the 
Republic of Turkey will be gradually shifted from n et foreign assets to net 
domestic assets until the next Monetary Policy Comm ittee meeting. To this 
end, only one intraday foreign exchange selling auc tion will be held on the 
days in which funding is  provided from the policy rate within the limits set  by 
the Monetary Policy Committee. 
 
Auction amount will be minimum USD 150 Million. Auc tion amount will 
be posted on CBTQ at 15:00. Full amount of offers r eceived will be met up to 
the auctioned amount and the same guidelines stated  for “Intraday Foreign 
Exchange Selling Auctions in the CBRT’s Press Relea se” of 11 June 2013 will 
apply.
