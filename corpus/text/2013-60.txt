PRESS RELEASE ON GOVERNOR BAŞÇI’S PRESENTATION ON
“MONETARY POLICIES” IN GAZİANTEP

Governor Erdem Başçı will give a conference presentation on “Monetary
Policies” on Wednesday, 11 December 2013 at 10:00 hours at the Şehitkamil
Congress and Culture Center in Gaziantep.
The conference will be organized jointly by the Gaziantep Chamber of Industry
and Dünya Newspaper.
