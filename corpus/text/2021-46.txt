Briefing on Inflation Report 2021-IV will
be given on 28 October 2021 in Ankara
22 October 2021

No: 2021-46

Governor Şahap Kavcıoğlu will hold a briefing on Inflation Report 2021-IV at 10.30 a.m. on
Thursday, 28 October 2021 at the Head Office of the CBRT. The meeting will take place in person.
A live webcast of the Turkish-language briefing will be available on the Twitter
and YouTube accounts of the CBRT and with simultaneous English translation here on the
CBRT website.
Members of the press and economists who wish to follow the briefing should inform the Bank
by 2 p.m. on Wednesday, 27 October 2021, via an e-mail to: basin@tcmb.gov.tr.
Briefing Program:
10.30 a.m. – 11.00 a.m.: Governor Şahap Kavcıoğlu’s presentation on Inflation Report 2021-IV
11.00 a.m. – 11.30 a.m.: Q&A session

Contact
For further information, you may send an e-mail to basin@tcmb.gov.tr.
