Press Release on Changes to Structure 
of Investment Tendency Statistics  
26 April  2021 No: 2021 -19  
 
Effective from 26 April  2021 , the Investment Tendency Statistics are gaining a new structure.   
 
The Investment Survey is conducted by the CBRT in full compliance with the “Joint Harmonised 
European Union Programme of Business and Consumer Surveys”, and the survey’s structure has 
been changed to conform with revisions made in the programme. Informatio n regarding the 
changes is accessible through the Metadata and Methodological Changes  headings on the CBRT  
website Invest ment Tendency Statistics page.   
The new series of the Investment Tendency Statistics have been calculated in accordance with 
the new method so as to cover previous periods and have been revised as of the 2009 Spring 
period. Th e revised data are accessible on the Investment Tendency Statistics page .
