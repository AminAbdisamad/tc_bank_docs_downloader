Food and Agricultural Product Markets Monitoring an d 
Evaluation Committee Convened  
The Food and Agricultural Product Markets Monitorin g and Evaluation Committee (the Committee) 
met under the presidency of Deputy Prime Minister M ehmet Şimşek. A summary of the agenda and 
decisions is given below. 
• Work on the technical infrastructure regarding the Early Warning System, intended to 
support stable supply and price formation for food products, was presented to the 
Committee and a framework for cooperation between t he involved institutions was 
designed. 
• Proposals for alternative models on flexible foreig n trade policies were evaluated, which will 
work in tandem with the Early Warning System, safeg uard the sector’s interests, and step in, 
when necessary, to stabilize the supply and prices of food products; related institutions were 
authorized to carry through the necessary work.  
• Endeavors to establish a road map and action plan f or taking logistical measures to reduce 
product loss rates, strengthening the producer orga nizations, regulating competition 
conditions in wholesale and retail trade, developin g private agricultural banking and 
reviewing the tax and incentive schemes in the agri cultural sector were decided to be 
finalized. 
• A framework for the Committee’s institutional struc ture and communication strategy was 
determined.
