Number: 2005 -26 
29 June 2005  
 
 
THE CBRT PRESS RELEASE  
 
 
As of 1 July 2005, “New Romanian Leu” will be put into circulation by dropping four zeros 
from “Leu” which is the Romanian legal tender. Starting from that date, the exchange rates on 
“Romanian Leu” banknotes that are currently  announced daily by the Central Bank of Turkey for 
“100 Romanian Lei” in the list of the “CBRT Indicative Exchange Rates”  will be announced for “1 
New Romanian Leu”.
