PRESS RELEASE ON THE CONFERENCE ON  
“POLICY RESPONSES TO COMMODITY PRICE MOVEMENTS”  
TO BE ORGANIZED BY THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC  OF TURKEY, 
IMF AND IMF ECONOMIC REVIEW 
 
 
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT), the International 
Monetary Fund (IMF), and the IMF Economic Review ar e organizing a conference on 
“Policy Responses to Commodity Price Movements”. Th e conference will be hosted 
by the CBRT and will take place in Istanbul on Apri l 6-7, 2012. 
The deadline for call for papers  or proposals is October 28, 2011. Further 
information on the conference program will be poste d on our website.
