Press Release on Market Developments
7 August 2020, No: 2020-42
Liquidity limits offered to Primary Dealers in the framework of Open Market Operations (OMOs)
will be reduced to half of their current limits effective from 10 August 2020. The CBRT will provide
banks with detailed information about the new limits.
