PRESS RELEASE  
13 November 2014  
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey Starts Publishing 
Balance of Payments Statistics in accordance with BPM6 . 
Starting with the Balance of Payments Statistics for September 2014, balance of 
payments data will be published in accordance with the new definitions and methods 
set out in the sixth edition of the Balance of Payments and International Investment 
Position  Manual (BPM6). In this context, the balance of payments statistics have been 
revised backwards so as to conform to BPM6 presentation, and historical data for 
“Analytical Presentation” and “Detailed Presentation” have been provided beginning 
from 1975 and 1984, respectively.  
For comparison purposes, both the old and the new presentations of balance of 
payments statistics will be provided to users until the dissemination of March 2015 
data.  
Information on the related changes can be found in the “ Methodology ” note as well 
as the “ Note on the Changeover to the 6th Edition of the Balance of Payments and 
International Investment Position Manual (BPM6) ” published on 14 August 2014 to 
inform the public in advance. Both documents are accessible at the Internet address 
http://www.tcmb.gov.tr  under “Statistics/Balance of Payments and Rel ated 
Statistics/Balance of Payments Statistics”.
