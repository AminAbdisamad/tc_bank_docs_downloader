CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEASE
Governor Erdem Başçı delivered a presentation to Council of Ministers.
Governor Erdem Başçı delivered the first presentation of 2014 to the Council of
ministers on Monday, 02 June 2014 at 1:00 p.m.
Governor Başçı emphasized the following points:
•

The pace of GDP growth is around 4 percent.

•

Inflation is expected to begin its descent as of the June CPI data.

•

Loan growth rates are approaching 15 percent.

•

The improvement in the current account deficit is expected to continue.
