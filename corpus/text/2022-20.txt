Press Release on the Issue of TRY 5
Banknotes of E9 Series, Version VI
28 March 2022

No: 2022-20

As per the authorization given to the Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye by Law No. 1211, the
E9 series, version VI banknotes in denomination of TRY 5 will be put into circulation as of 28
March 2022.
Compared to TRY 5 banknotes of E9 series, version I, II, III, IV and V in circulation, the signatures
on TRY 5 banknotes of version VI have been rearranged to include Governor Prof. Şahap
Kavcıoğlu’s and Deputy Governor Taha Çakmak’s signatures.
Except for the signatures, the new banknotes will be identical to version I, II, III, IV and V
banknotes with regard to their dimensions, obverse and reverse compositions, general features
and appearances.
Version I, II, III, IV, V and VI banknotes will be in circulation concurrently.
