Press Release on Reserve Requirements
18 January 2019, No: 2019-03
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey decided to exclude deposits/participation funds of
official institutions from the liabilities subject to reserve requirements. Additionally, considering
the significant decline in the liabilities under Provisional Article 6 of the Communiqué on Reserve
Requirements, the relevant article has been revoked.
