24 May 2005

PRESS ANNOUNCEMENT REGARDING THE INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE
HELD BETWEEN 16-18 MAY 2005 IN ISTANBUL HOSTED BY THE CENTRAL
BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY
An international Conference titled “Financial Stability and Implications of Basel II”,
hosted by the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey, was held in Istanbul between 16-18
May 2005. Presentations and speeches regarding the Conference can be reached from the
Conference website, the address of which is http://www2.tcmb.gov.tr/conference. Also,
Conference Proceedings will be published in both Turkish and English.
