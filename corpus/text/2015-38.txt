Press Release on Reserve Requirements  
The coverage of liabilities subject to reserve requ irements of foreign branches of 
banks incorporated in Turkey has been broadened. Wi th this regulation, foreign 
branches of banks will also be encouraged to borrow  longer term. 
Since the implementation will apply to foreign bran ches’ liabilities emerging after 29 
May 2015, this regulation will not have any notewor thy impact on the financial 
position of the banks.
