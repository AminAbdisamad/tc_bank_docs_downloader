Press Release on Election of New
Monetary Policy Committee Member
8 April 2021

No: 2021-15

As per Article 22/A of the Law on the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey, Prof. Elif Haykır
Hobikoğlu, who is a member of the Board, has been elected as a Monetary Policy Committee
member.
Prof. Hobikoğlu will join the Monetary Policy Committee meeting to be held on Thursday, 15 April
2021.
