Press Release on Foreign Exchange 
Deposits against Turkish Lira Deposits  
9 May  2018, No: 201 8-14 
 
The daily amount of  Foreign Exchange Deposits against Turkish Lira Deposits auctions  has been 
increased from 1,25 b illion US Dollars to 1,5  billion US Dollars.   
 
Accordingly , the maximum total outstanding deposit amount in the auctions,  which is currently 
6,25 b illion  US Dollars , may reach up to 7,5 billion US Dollars .
