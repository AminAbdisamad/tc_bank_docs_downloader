International Photography Contest of the Central Ba nk of the 
Republic of Turkey concludes 
The photography contest organized for the first tim e this year by the Central Bank of 
the Republic of Turkey has concluded. The contest o n the theme “Economy and the 
Human Being” was managed by the Photographic Arts S ociety (FSK) with the support 
of the Federation Internationale de L’Art Photograp hique (International Photographic 
Arts Federation) and the Photographic Arts Federati on of Turkey, and 1,362 artists 
from 30 countries entered the contest with a total of 4,781 photographs.  
The dates for the award ceremony and exhibition of award-winning photographs will 
be announced at a later date.
