 PRESS RELEASE ON 
“THE CONTEST FOR ACADEMIC STUDIES” TO BE ORGANIZED BY  
THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY 
 The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will or ganize “The Third Contest 
for Academic Studies”  with a view to supporting academic studies in the field of the 
Turkish economy or central banking, and making thes e studies accessible to a larger 
audience. 
The deadline for applications is close of business on Monday, 20 February 
2012. Please click on the “ Application Form ”, and for further information about the
