Press Release on the 86th Ordinary
General Assembly Meeting of the CBRT
30 March 2018, No: 2018-07
Ordinary General Assembly Meeting of the CBRT will be held on 12 April 2018.
The 86th Ordinary General Assembly Meeting of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will
be held on Thursday, 12 April 2018 at 2:00 pm at the Conference Hall of the CBRT Head Office.
