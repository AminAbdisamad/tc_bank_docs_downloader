PRESS RELEASE ON THE FINANCIAL CONTRIBUTION TO THE 
INTERNATIONAL MONETARY FUND  
  
  
At the G- 20 meeting held in Los Cabos, a number of emerging market countries, 
including Turkey, declared their intention to contribute to global financial stability by 
increasing the resources of the International Monetary Fund (IMF).  
Within the framework of the IMF’s press release of 15 June 2012 and No. 12/229 , the 
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will contribute to the IMF resources up to USD 5 billion, to be counted as part of its international reserves.
