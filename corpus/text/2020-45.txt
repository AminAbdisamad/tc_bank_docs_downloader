Press Release on Turkish Lira Liquidity
Management
18 August 2020, No: 2020-45
In the framework of the tightening steps taken under liquidity management, banks’ borrowing
limits at the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) Interbank Money Market for O/N
transactions will be reduced to half of their current limits effective from 19 August 2020.
