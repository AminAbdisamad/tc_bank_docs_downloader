26 December 2015 
Press Release on the Redemption Period for E7 Emiss ion Group 
Banknotes  
It was announced in Official Gazette No. 26028 date d 19 December 2005 that the 
Seventh Emission Group (E7) banknotes with denomina tions of 250,000; 500,000; 
1,000,000; 5,000,000; 10,000,000 and 20,000,000 wou ld be withdrawn from 
circulation as of 1 January 2006. The 10-year redem ption period for these banknotes 
will end on 31 December 2015, close of business. 
Accordingly, the said banknotes may be exchanged at  the branches of both the 
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey and T.C. Zir aat Bankası until the close of 
business on 31 December 2015. The E7 banknotes will  lose their value as of 1 January 
2016.
