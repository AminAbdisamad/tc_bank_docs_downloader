CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY
PRESS RELEASE

The exhibition titled “Aesthetic Attitude and Creative Thinking in Contemporary
Turkish Painting”, featuring works from the Art Collection of the Central Bank of the
Republic of Turkey, will be held between 11 - 31 October 2013 in Ufa, Russia, at the
National Museum of the Republic of Bashkortostan.
The opening ceremony of the exhibition will take place on Friday, 11 October
2013 at 12:00 hours, in the presence of Mr. Necati Şahin, the Deputy Governor of the
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey; Mr. Sabri Tunç Angılı, the Consul General of
the Republic of Turkey in Kazan; and Mr. Valiullin Gali, the Director of the National
Museum of the Republic of Bashkortostan.
