Press Release on the Issue of 50 Turkish
Lira Banknotes of E9 Series, Version IV
20 March 2020, No: 2020-18
As per the authorization given to the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey by Law No. 1211, the
E9 series, version IV banknotes in denomination of 50 will be put into circulation as of 23 March
2020.
Compared to previous versions, the signatures on the 50 TL banknotes of version IV have been
rearranged to include Governor Murat Uysal’s and Deputy Governor Murat Çetinkaya’s
signatures.
Except for the signatures, the new banknotes will be identical to version I, II and III banknotes
with regard to their dimensions, obverse and reverse compositions, general features and
appearances.
Version I, II, III and IV banknotes will be in circulation concurrently. An announcement regarding
version IV 100 and 10 TL banknotes will be made at a later date.
