Press Release on Turkish Lira Liquidity
Management
24 August 2020, No: 2020-53
In the framework of the tightening steps taken under liquidity management, a repo auction of TRY
10 billion has been conducted via the traditional auction method. The maturity of this repo
auction has been determined as 23 September 2020.
In the upcoming period, traditional repo auctions may continue on the days deemed necessary,
and details regarding auctions will be published through markets data distribution systems.
Moreover, due to the increased share of traditional repo auctions within the scope of the liquidity
management, on the days deemed necessary, the amount of funding provided by the CBRT
through Borsa Istanbul repo markets may be reduced.
