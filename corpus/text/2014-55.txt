THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEASE
Governor Erdem Başçı delivered a presentation in Kastamonu.
Governor Erdem Başçı delievered a presentation in the meeting held by Kastamonu
Chamber of Commerce and Industry on Thursday, 11 September 2014 at 10:00 a.m.
Governor Başçı emphasized the following points:
•

Turkey’s share in world exports has been increasing.

•

Net exports contribute positively to growth and rebalancing.

•

The Central Bank supports exports through rediscount credits.

•

Inflation is expected to decline after temporary increase in food prices lose its
effect.

•

Tight monetary stance will be maintained until there is a significant
improvement in the inflation outlook.
