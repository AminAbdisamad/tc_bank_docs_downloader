CBRT Blog Opens to Public 
The CBRT Blog, developed to share and discuss the views of the Central Bank of the 
Republic of Turkey’s staff with the public and make timely contributions to the 
economic agenda, has now been launched.  
The blog can be followed at  www.tcmbblog.org/en .
