PRESS RELEASE
21 November 2017

Press Release On Turkish Lira Liquidity Management
Banks’ borrowing limits at the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT)
Interbank Money Market for O/N transactions have been reduced to zero to be
effective as of 22 November 2017.
To be effective from the same date, banks’ limit for the intraday liquidity facility will
be increased to twice the size of Interbank Money Market borrowing limits valid on
21 November 2017.
