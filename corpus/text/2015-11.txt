G20 Central Bank Governors will Meet in Istanbul to Evaluate
Macroprudential Policy Experiences
Upon the invitation of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey, the G20 Central
Bank Governors will convene at a meeting organized jointly with the Bank for
International Settlements (BIS) on 9 February 2015 at 15:00 hours, at the Istanbul
Congress Center.
The meeting will be devoted to evaluating country experiences on macroprudential
policies. The meeting will be closed to the press except for photo and video shooting
at the opening.
