PRESS RELEASE ON FX LIQUIDITY
As of 13 March 2015, foreign exchange (FX) liquidity of approximately USD 1.5 billion
will be provided to the system via adjustments in the reserve option coefficients.
On 27 March 2015, approximately USD 1.3 billion will be added to Central Bank
reserves via measured increases in reserve requirement ratios for the non-core shortterm FX liabilities.
Thereby, FX liquidity for the banking system together with incentives for longer term
borrowing will be provided.
