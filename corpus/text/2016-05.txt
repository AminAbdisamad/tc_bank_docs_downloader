PRESS RELEASE
22 January 2016

Briefing on Inflation Report 2016-I will be given on 26 January
2016 in Ankara.
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report
2016-I" on Tuesday, 26 January 2016 and Governor Erdem Başçı will hold a briefing
on the report at 10:30 a.m. the same day at the Conference Hall of the CBRT in
Ankara.
Governor Başçı's speech on "Inflation Report 2015-IV" can be accessed here.
For previous issues of the Inflation Report, please see: Publications/Reports/Inflation
Report.
