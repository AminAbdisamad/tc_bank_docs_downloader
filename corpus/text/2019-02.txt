Press Release on Extraordinary General
Assembly Meeting of the CBRT
18 January 2019, No: 2019-02
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey held an Extraordinary General Assembly Meeting on
18 January 2019 in Ankara.
An amendment was made to the Articles of Association of the Bank, allowing the General
Assembly to convene within three months following the end of the respective accounting period
each year.
In addition, an advance dividend was decided to be paid and accordingly, the distribution of an
amount totalling 33.7 billion TL to shareholders has started in line with the related legislation.
