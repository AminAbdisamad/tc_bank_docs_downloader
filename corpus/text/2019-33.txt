Press Release on Turkish Lira Currency
Swap Market
5 August 2019, No: 2019-33
In the press release dated 31 August 2018, the CBRT announced that the Turkish Lira Currency Swap
Market was opened at the Central Bank and the transactions would be conducted via quotation
method with banks authorised to conduct transactions at the Foreign Exchange and Banknotes
Market within their pre-determined limits.
With the aim of enhancing flexibility in Turkish lira and FX liquidity management, Turkish lira
currency swap transactions, which are currently executed with 1 week maturity via quotation
method in the aforementioned market, will also be executed with 1, 3 and 6 month maturities via
the traditional auction (multiple-price) method. The details of the auctions to be held on days
deemed necessary will be announced at 10:00 via data vendors.
The transactions to be conducted via quotation and auction methods will be carried out within the
total limit currently set for the transactions carried out via quotation method.
