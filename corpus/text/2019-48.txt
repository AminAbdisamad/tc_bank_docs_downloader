Redemption period for E8 Series New 
Turkish Lira banknotes will expire on 31 
December 2019  
9 December  2019, No: 2019 -48 
 
Official Gazette No. 26516 dated 8 May 2007 announced that E8 Series New Turkish Lira 
banknotes in denominations of 1, 5, 10, 20, 50 and 100 would be withdrawn from circula tion on 1 
January 2010 and their 10 -year  redemption period would expire  on 31 December 2019.  
 
Accordingly, these banknotes can be replaced before  close of business on 31 December 2019  at 
Central Bank  and Ziraat Bank branches and will become invalid after 1 January 2020 .
