CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEASE
A Briefing on Inflation Report 2014-II will be given on 30 April 2014 in İstanbul.
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will publish its Inflation Report
2014-II on Wednesday, 30 April 2014 and Governor Erdem Başçı will hold a briefing
on the report at 10:00 a.m. the same day at the Renaissance Polat Hotel in İstanbul.
Venue: Renaissance Polat İstanbul Hotel
Sahil Yolu Cad. No: 2
Yeşilyurt – 34149
İstanbul
