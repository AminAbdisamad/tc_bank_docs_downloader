“Oversight Framework on Payment and Securities Settlement
Systems” Published
The “Oversight Framework on Payment and Securities Settlement Systems”
governing the oversight activities of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey on
payment and securities settlement systems within the scope of the Law No: 6493 on
Payment and Securities Settlement Systems, Payment Services and Electronic Money
Institutions has been published.
The “Oversight Framework on Payment and Securities Settlement Systems” specifies
the scope and method of oversight activities conducted by the Central Bank of the
Republic of Turkey on the payment and securities settlement systems established in
Turkey.
