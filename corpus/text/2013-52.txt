CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY
PRESS RELEASE

The exhibition titled “Ottoman Sultans in the Art of Ebru”, featuring works from
the Art Collection of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT), will be open
to the public from 1 October 2013 to 12 December 2013 at the Art Gallery of the
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey, in Ankara.

The exhibition, which will host a series of “ebru” (Turkish paper marbling)
portraits of the “36 Ottoman Sultans” created by the ebru master Sadreddin Özçimi,
will be opened by the CBRT Governor, Mr. Erdem Başçı on Tuesday, 1 October 2013
at 18:30 hours.

Address: The Art Gallery of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey
