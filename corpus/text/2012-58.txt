PRESS RELEASE ON
THE MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING WITH THE NATIONAL BANK OF
TAJIKISTAN

A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed between the
Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey and the National Bank of Tajikistan in a
ceremony held on 18 December 2012 in Ankara in presence of President of Turkey
Abdullah Gül and President of Tajikistan İmamali Rahman.
The MoU lays the ground for continued cooperation in the field of central
banking between the two central banks.
