PRESS RELEASE

Briefing on Inflation Report 2016-IV will be given on 27 October
2016 in Istanbul
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) will publish its "Inflation Report 2016-IV" on
Thursday, 27 October 2016 and Governor Murat Çetinkaya will hold a briefing on the report at 10:30
a.m. the same day in Istanbul.
A live webcast of the presentation will be available here.
