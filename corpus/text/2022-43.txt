Briefing on Inflation Report 2022-IV will
be given on 27 October 2022 in Ankara
21 October 2022

No: 2022-43

Governor Şahap Kavcıoğlu will hold a briefing on Inflation Report 2022-IV at 10.30 a.m. on
Thursday, 27 October 2022 at the Head Office of the CBRT. The meeting will take place in person.
A live webcast of the Turkish-language briefing will be available on the Twitter and YouTube
accounts of the CBRT and with simultaneous English translation here on the CBRT website.
Members of the press and economists who wish to follow the briefing should inform the Bank
by 2 p.m. on Wednesday, 26 October 2022, via an e-mail to: basin@tcmb.gov.tr.
Briefing Program:
10.30 a.m. – 11.00 a.m.: Governor Şahap Kavcıoğlu’s presentation on Inflation Report 2022-IV
11.00 a.m. – 11.30 a.m.: Q&A session

Contact
For further information, you may send an e-mail to basin@tcmb.gov.tr.
