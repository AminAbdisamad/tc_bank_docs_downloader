Press Release on CBRT’s Shareholding in
Interbank Card Center
30 April 2020, No: 2020-27
A provision was added as Paragraph 4 to Article 8 of the Law No. 6493 on Payment and Securities
Settlement Systems, Payment Services, Payment Institutions and Electronic Money Institutions by
the Law No. 7192 of 12 November 2019. This provision authorized the CBRT to be a shareholder
of any systemically important system operators established and to be established to ensure
uninterrupted operation of the payment and securities settlement systems.
The Interbank Card Center (BKM) operates pursuant to the license obtained from the CBRT and is
considered as a system operator with systemic importance for the payment systems in Turkey
due to the operations it conducts.
The CBRT has identified significant areas to improve in the payments infrastructure and
innovative business methodologies. Within the context of actions to be taken in this regard, in
addition to its current roles with a focus on payments with cards, the BKM is planned to
undertake significant tasks.
To this end, the CBRT has become the controlling shareholder of the BKM in accordance with
Paragraph 4, Article 8 of the Law No. 6493.
