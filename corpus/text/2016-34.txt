PRESS RELEASE
9 August 2016

Press Release on Reserve Requirements
Reserve requirement ratios and reserve option coefficients have been changed as
follows.
• Turkish lira reserve requirement ratios have been decreased by 50 basis points
for all maturity brackets.
• In the context of Reserve Options Mechanism, coefficients for the second,
third and fourth tranches of the FX facility and for the first two tranches of the
gold facility have been increased by 0.1.
Should the reserve option utilization rates remain unchanged, approximately 1.1
billion Turkish liras and 600 million US dollars of liquidity would be provided to the
financial system with these changes.
