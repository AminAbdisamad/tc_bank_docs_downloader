Press Release On The 87th Ordinary
General Assembly Meeting of the CBRT
1 March 2019, No: 2019-09
Ordinary General Assembly Meeting of the CBRT will be held on 15 March 2019.
The 87th Ordinary General Assembly Meeting of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will be
held on Friday, 15 March 2019 at 3:00 pm at the Conference Hall of the CBRT Head Office.
