Press Release Regarding the Change in 
Data Release Calendar  
18 August  2021 No: 2021-36 
 
Short Term External Debt Statistics, International Investment Position, Foreign Exchange 
Assets and Liabilities of Non -Financial Companies and Securities Statistics will be revised on 
19 August 2021.  
 
To ensure simultaneous announcement of the upcoming revisions , the current dissemination 
dates in the Data Release Calendar of the  International Investment Position, the Foreign 
Exchange Assets and Liabilities of Non -Financial Companies  and Securities Statistics have been 
changed. These statistics will be released at 10 a.m. on Thursday, 19 August 2021.   
Detailed information on the revisions to be made can be accessed from the section s of “Future 
Revisions”  in the Short Term Externa l Debt Statistics , International Investment Position , Foreign 
Exchange Assets and Liabilities of Non -Financial Companies  and Securities Statistics  pages.
