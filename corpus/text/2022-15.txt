Press Release on the Issue of T RY 100 
and TRY 50 Banknotes of E9 Series, 
Version V  
23 February  2022 No: 202 2-15 
 
As per the authorization given to the Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye  by Law No. 1211, the 
E9 series, version V banknotes  in denomination s of TRY 100 and TRY 50 will be put into 
circulation as of 23 February  2022. 
Compared to TRY 100 and TRY 50 banknotes of E9 series, version I, II, III and IV in circulation,  the 
signatures on TRY 100 and TRY 50 banknotes of version V have been rearranged to include 
Governor Prof. Şahap Kavcıoğlu ’s signature , and the signatures of the following authorities in the 
respective order:  
• Deputy Governor Mustafa Duman,  
• Deputy Governor Taha Çakmak.   
Except for the signatures, the new banknotes will be identical to version I, II, III and IV banknotes 
with regard to their dimensions, obverse and reverse compositions, general features and 
appearances.  
Version I, II, III, I V and V banknotes will be in circulation concurrently.
