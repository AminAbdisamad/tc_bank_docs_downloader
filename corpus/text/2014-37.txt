THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE REPUBLIC OF TURKEY PRESS RELEASE
Governor Erdem Başçı delivered a speech in Istanbul.
Governor Erdem Başçı delivered a speech in “Sustainable Mega Cities and Financial
Centers from New York to Istanbul Conference” on Friday, 13 June 2014 at 10.00
hours.
Governor Başçı emphasized the following points:
•

Inflation is expected to begin its descent as of June CPI data.

•

Loan growth rates are approaching 15%.

•

The improvement in current account deficit is expected to continue.

•

The pace of GDP growth is around 4 percent.

•

A healty financial sector and prudent macroeconomic policies contribute to the
sustainable growth in Turkey.
