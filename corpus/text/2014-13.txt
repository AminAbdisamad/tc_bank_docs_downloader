PRESS RELEASE ON THE METHODOLOGICAL CHANGES IN COMPOSITE
LEADING INDICATOR (MBONCU-SUE)

The methodology for the construction of composite leading indicators, published
on our web site via “Data / Statistical Data / Composite Leading Indicator (MBONCUSUE)”, has been revised in order to be accordant with the methodology of OECD.
Starting from 14 March 2014, the new MBONCU-SUE, constructed with new
methodology, will continue to be published on a monthly basis and will be accessible
via same link. Detailed information about methodological changes can be found on
our web site via “Publications / Research / CBT Research Notes in Economics /
Methodological Changes in Composite Leading Indicator for Turkish Economic
Activity”.
