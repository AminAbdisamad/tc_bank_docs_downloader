Press Release on Market Developments   
6 August 2020, No: 2020-41 
 
Fiscal and monetary policies succeeded in containing the effects of the pandemic on the Turkish 
economy  and maintaining the productive capacity . Recent d ata suggest that economic recovery 
has gained pace. In this context, with  economic activity normalizing as of early August, the 
targeted additional liquidity facilities will be phased out . 
Meanwhile, price developments in the markets are being closely monitored. The Central Bank will 
use all available instruments to reduce the excessive volatility in the markets in line with the price stability and financial stability objectives.
