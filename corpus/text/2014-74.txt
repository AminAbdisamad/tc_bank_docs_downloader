PRESS RELEASE
5 December 2014

The Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy for 2015 will be
announced in a Press Conference on December 10, 2014
The Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey will release the Monetary and Exchange
Rate Policy for 2015 on Wednesday, 10 December 2014.
Governor Erdem Başçı will present the policy text at a press conference to be held at
10:30 a.m. the same day at the Conference Hall of the CBRT in Ankara.
The previous Monetary and Exchange Rate Policy Texts are available on the CBRT
website.
