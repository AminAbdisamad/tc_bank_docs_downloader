Release Date: 29 January 2013

PRESS RELEASE ON THE PUBLICATION OF
THE “CONSUMER TENDENCY SURVEY AND CONSUMER CONFIDENCE
INDEX”
The “Consumer Tendency Survey and Consumer Confidence Index”, which
used to be published on CBRT’s web site via “Data/Surveys/Consumer Tendency
Survey and Consumer Confidence Index”, started to be published, by 29 January
2013, in its revised version, on the Turkish Statistical Institute’s (Turkstat) web site
http://www.turkstat.gov.tr, The data for January 2013, the first in revised form, is
already accessible on the mentioned web site.
