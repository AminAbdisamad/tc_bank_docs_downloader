Press Release on the Reduction of Commission on Eur o 
Denominated Account Balances Held with the Central Bank 
The annual commission rate of 20 basis point starte d to be applied with the press 
release dated 23 January 2015 on balances denominat ed in Euro in required reserves 
and notice accounts held with the Central Bank is r educed temporarily to 5 basis 
points due to the recent developments in the Eurozo ne.  
1)  The commission rate charged on the above mentioned account balances held 
by banks and finance companies may be reviewed when  deemed necessary by 
taking global and local financial market conditions  into account.  
2)  The new commission rate will be effective from 1 Ju ly 2015. 
3)  Commission rate will be announced on a daily basis through the Anadolu 
Agency DV008, Reuters CBTB and Bloomberg CBT pages.
