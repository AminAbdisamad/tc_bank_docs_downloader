PRESS RELEASE
30 June 2015

Press Release on the Reduction of Commission on Euro
Denominated Account Balances Held with the Central Bank
The annual commission rate of 20 basis point started to be applied with the press
release dated 23 January 2015 on balances denominated in Euro in required reserves
and notice accounts held with the Central Bank is reduced temporarily to 5 basis
points due to the recent developments in the Eurozone.
1) The commission rate charged on the above mentioned account balances held
by banks and finance companies may be reviewed when deemed necessary by
taking global and local financial market conditions into account.
2) The new commission rate will be effective from 1 July 2015.
3) Commission rate will be announced on a daily basis through the Anadolu
Agency DV008, Reuters CBTB and Bloomberg CBT pages.
