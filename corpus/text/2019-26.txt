Press Release o n the Maximum Interest 
Rates for Credit Cards in Q 3 2019 
29 June  2019, No: 201 9-26 
 
The monthly maximum contractual interest rate for the credit card borrowings has been revised 
as 2.00 percent for the Turkish lira and 1. 60 percent for foreign exchange transactions, whereas 
the monthly maximum overdue interest rate will be 2. 40 percent for the Turkish lira and 2. 00 
percent for foreign exchange transactions, to be effective from 1 July 2019 . 
 
The CBRT advises credit card holders with short -term credit needs to seek a consumer loan rather 
than borrowing on personal credit cards, by taking into account the factors such  as pricing and 
maturity.
