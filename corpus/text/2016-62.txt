Food and Agricultural Product Markets Monitoring an d 
Evaluation Committee Convened  
The Food and Agricultural Product Markets Monitorin g and Evaluation Committee 
(the Committee) met under the presidency of Deputy Prime Minister Mehmet 
Şimşek. A summary of the agenda and decisions made is given below. 
• The progress registered so far regarding the Early Warning System, which is 
projected to be established to support stable suppl y and price formation for 
food products, was evaluated. The Committee decided  that studies on the 
establishment of the system would be conducted unde r the coordination of 
the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey and the Ministry of Food, 
Agriculture and Livestock and within this scope, wo rks on the technical 
infrastructure would be completed as soon as possib le. 
• The Ministry of Customs and Trade presented informa tion on taking logistical 
measures to reduce product loss rates, strengthenin g the producer 
organizations, and regulating competition condition s in wholesale and retail 
trade and the detailed action plan was reviewed.  
• An evaluation was made of the application principle s on flexible foreign trade 
policies which will work in tandem with the Early W arning System, safeguard 
the sector’s interests, and step in, when necessary , to stabilize the supply and 
prices of food products. 
• A presentation was delivered by the authorities of Istanbul Metropolitan 
Municipality to set an example for the studies that  can be conducted by local 
administrations.  
• The agenda and the work schedule of the Committee w ere reviewed.
