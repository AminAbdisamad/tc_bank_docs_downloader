PRESS RELEASE
24 July 2015

Press Release on Foreign Exchange Deposit Interest Rates
1) Due to recent global and local developments, USD deposit rate at one week
maturity has been reduced from 3.5 percent to 3 percent.
2) The new rate will be effective from July 27, 2015. At this rate banking sector will
be able to borrow from the Central Bank at one week maturity.
3) When deemed necessary, without any need for a press release these rates for the
USD and EUR will be adjusted in either direction at 9:30 a.m. on business days, and
will be valid for the same business day. The rates will be announced through
Anadolu Agency DV006, Reuters CBTJ and Bloomberg CBT pages.
